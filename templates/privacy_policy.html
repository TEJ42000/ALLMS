Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: This Privacy Policy explains how ALLMS (AI-Powered Learning Management System) collects, uses, and protects your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

ALLMS
Email: privacy@allms.example.com
Data Protection Officer: dpo@allms.example.com

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • User ID (automatically generated)
  • Account creation date

2.2 Learning Data

  • Quiz results and scores
  • Course progress and completion status
  • Flashcard study sessions
  • Assessment submissions

2.3 AI Tutor Interactions

  • Questions asked to the AI tutor
  • AI-generated responses
  • Conversation context and history
  • Timestamps of interactions

2.4 Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Session data
  • Cookies (see Cookie Policy)

2.5 Uploaded Materials

  • Documents you upload for study purposes
  • File metadata (name, size, upload date)

3. How We Use Your Data

3.1 Essential Services (Legal Basis: Contract Performance)

  • Provide access to the learning platform
  • Authenticate your account
  • Store your learning progress
  • Generate quizzes and assessments

3.2 AI-Powered Features (Legal Basis: Consent)

  • Provide AI tutoring services via Anthropic Claude API
  • Generate personalized study materials
  • Analyze learning patterns to improve recommendations

3.3 Service Improvement (Legal Basis: Legitimate Interest)

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the platform
  • Monitor system performance and errors
  • Conduct security monitoring

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 Anthropic (Claude API)

When you use AI tutoring features, your questions and conversation context are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

4.2 Google Cloud Platform

We use Google Cloud Firestore to store your data. Google's GDPR compliance: https://cloud.google.com/privacy/gdpr

4.3 No Data Selling

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Your GDPR Rights

5.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all your personal data. Visit your Privacy Dashboard to download your data.

5.2 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data. Visit your Privacy Dashboard to initiate deletion.

5.3 Right to Rectification

You can update your personal information in your account settings.

5.4 Right to Data Portability

You can export your data in JSON format from your Privacy Dashboard.

5.5 Right to Object

You can object to certain data processing activities by adjusting your privacy settings.

5.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for AI tutoring and analytics at any time in your privacy settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion:

  • Soft Delete: Data is marked for deletion and retained for 30 days
  • Permanent Deletion: After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted
  • Legal Obligations: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest (Google Cloud default encryption)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Audit logging of sensitive operations

8. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Authentication and session management (required)
  • Functional: Remember your preferences (optional)
  • Analytics: Understand how you use the platform (optional)

You can manage cookie preferences in the cookie consent banner.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the EU. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) with third-party processors
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
  • GDPR-compliant data processing agreements

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. If you are under 16, please obtain parental consent before using the platform.

11. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours as required by GDPR.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on the platform.
